Who We Are

Peaceful, Zero-Energy Homes — Within Your Budget

"Our goal is to build peaceful, sustainable living spaces that eliminate energy costs—all at a price that fits your budget."

There are countless construction and design companies to choose from, but what sets us apart at Sustreia is our commitment to environmental, social, and economic sustainability. While a beautiful design is essential, it's not the only factor to consider in building your dream home. Budget, sustainability, energy efficiency, and traditional Vastu principles all play crucial roles.

Our approach goes beyond design and budget; we integrate these values into every project to create spaces that support well-being and environmental harmony. Inspired by the United Nations' 17 Sustainable Development Goals — particularly Sustainable Cities and Communities and Affordable and Clean Energy — we take responsibility as proud Sri Lankans in promoting cleaner living, reducing domestic waste, and contributing to a sustainable future for our community.

Our Vision

To pioneer affordable, zero-energy-cost homes that harmonize sustainability, peace, and innovation—creating living spaces that respect nature, nurture well-being, and empower communities to thrive.

Our Mission

To design and build affordable, sustainable homes that eliminate energy costs, promote peaceful living, and embrace modern aesthetics—combining innovative thinking, expert knowledge, and traditional principles to deliver spaces that support well-being, respect the environment, and fulfill each client's unique needs.
